Seminar in Trieste: Giovanni Panti

Tuesday 4th October at 17:00 (ICTP) “A geometric model for the natural extension of continued fraction maps” Abstract: Just as classical “Floor” continued fractions induce the Gauss map on the real unit interval, other euclidean division algorithms (Ceiling, Even, Odd, NearestInteger, …) induce piecewise-fractional maps with integer coefficients. All these maps have natural extensions that can easily be described on an ad hoc basis. We present a general framework for dealing with all these maps in a uniform way.
