This page contains a record of the past editions of the

DinAmicI: Another Internet Seminar
(a.k.a. DAI Seminar)

a series of online seminars on Dynamical Systems and their applications.

Most seminars can be replayed on the DinAmicI YouTube Channel.

Organizers: A. Bianchi, C. Bonanno, M. Lenci, M. Seri, A. Sorrentino

  • May 14, 2020, 5pm CET: Giulio Tiozzo (University of Toronto, Canada), Central limit theorems for counting measures in coarse negative curvature
  • May 21, 2020, 5pm CET: Martin Leguil (Université Paris-Sud 11, France), Some rigidity results for billiards and hyperbolic flows
  • May 28, 2020, 5pm CET: Francesco Cellarosi (Queen’s University, Canada), Rational Horocycle lifts and the tails of Quadratic Weyl sums
  • June 4, 2020, 5pm CET: Sandro Vaienti (Centre de Physique Théorique, Marseille, France), Thermodynamic formalism for random weighted covering systems
  • June 10, 2020, 5pm CET: Seminar by Marta Maggioni postponed #ShutDownSTEM
  • June 17, 2020, 5pm CET: Sunrose Shrestha (Tufts University, USA), The topology and geometry of random square-tiled surfaces
  • June 24, 2020, 5pm CET: Andreas Knauf (Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg, Germany), Asymptotic velocity for scattering particles
  • July 1, 2020, 5pm CET: Marta Maggioni (Universiteit Leiden, Netherlands), Matching for random systems with an application to minimal weight expansions
  • July 8, 2020, 5pm CET: Françoise Pène (Université de Bretagne Occidentale, France), * Invariance by induction of the asymptotic variance*
